how to deal with the old calendar 如何处理旧日历

Direct Eco-Friendly Disposal

  • Paper Calendars: Tear off the metal staples/plastic seals → Dispose of in the recycling bin.

  • Calendar Bases: Cardboard or wooden bases can be recycled or reused separately.

👉 If you have a minimalist personality, this step is already perfect.

✂️ Craft Reuse

  • Cut out patterns for wrapping paper/envelopes (A thoughtful gift)

  • Collages/Mood Boards (Perfect for year-end reviews)

  • Bookmarks/Memo Paper (Cut them up to make new stationery)

  • Origami/Greeting Cards (Especially suitable for thick paper calendars)

📔 Emotional & Ritualistic Flow

  • Diary Material: Tear off a month's page and write down your memories of that month.

  • Year-End Archive: Choose 1-2 favorite pages to keep as a memento, and leave the rest alone.

  • Create a "Last Year's Memory Album": Photos + calendar pages, can be done in 5 minutes.

🖼️ Decorative

  • Frame a page as a small wall painting

  • Cut it into desk mats/drawer liners

  • Cut it into small sizes and paste it into journals or photo albums

🗑️ The Ultimate Solution

If seeing this only makes you think:

"Ugh, another thing to deal with"

Then—thank it for being with you for a year, and then recycle it.

Not everything needs to be "maximized"; ease is also important 🌿

直接环保处理

  • 纸质日历：撕掉金属钉/塑料封条 → 投进可回收
  • 台历底座：硬纸板或木头底座可单独回收或再利用
    👉 如果你是“断舍离型人格”，做到这一步已经很完美了。

✂️ 手工再利用

  • 剪图当包装纸 / 信封（送小礼物超有心意）
  • 拼贴画 / 情绪板（很适合做年度回顾）
  • 书签 / 便签纸（裁一裁就是新的文具）
  • 折纸 / 贺卡（厚纸质的日历尤其适合）

📔 情绪 & 仪式感流

  • 日记素材：把某个月撕下来，写下那个月的记忆
  • 年度存档：选 1–2 页最喜欢的留作纪念，其余放手
  • 做成“去年回忆册”：照片＋日历页，5 分钟就能完成

🖼️ 装饰型

  • 裱一页当墙面小画
  • 裁成 桌垫 / 抽屉垫纸
  • 剪成小尺寸贴在手账或相册里

🗑️ 终极方案

如果你看到它只感到：

「啊，又多一个东西要处理」
那就——感谢它陪你走过一年，然后回收掉。
不是所有东西都需要被“最大化利用”，轻松也很重要 🌿
