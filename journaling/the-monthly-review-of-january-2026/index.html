January：at the beginning of everything 一月：在一切尚未发生前

Today is the last day of January, and good news—the weather has finally cleared up after a long time!

I had my favorite oatmeal cup for breakfast, then spent an hour working out at noon—the gym alone is so therapeutic…

At the beginning of this month, my New Year's trip with Shanshan took us to Taizhou, a place I'd always wanted to visit. I ate some incredibly delicious seaweed pancakes—crispy on the outside, soft and chewy on the inside, my favorite sweet and savory flavor. Another surprise was the seaweed oatmeal pancakes; along with the initial delight, I felt a belated sense of envy: the people of Taizhou can eat such delicious food for breakfast every day! And let's not forget, we sampled countless variations of rice cake in the streets and alleys of Taizhou, haha. Shanshan concluded: it feels like we ate all the rice cakes we need for the year here. I feel completely innocent.

Remembering that last year's New Year's Eve was the first time Shanshan and I met, this New Year's trip felt especially meaningful. Besides the soft, sweet, and fragrant food, this trip was also a journey through strange and wonderful things. Every night, we stayed in our hotel room chasing progress, finally bringing the final season to a close on the last day of our trip back from Ningbo. Maybe when I occasionally watch this show in the future, I'll subconsciously think of the aroma of seaweed pancakes, haha.

Another piece of good news is that my design shop made its first sale this month! (Applause 👏) My first client was an American couple. The wife was the one who contacted me to discuss my design needs. Despite her slow replies and delayed payment links (which I've already reflected on), she was still completely uncritical of my design and quote… Sometimes I really can't believe my good luck—I must have unknowingly done something incredibly good to have such kind and tolerant clients.

Mid-month, I met Meng Han—an online friend I'd known for over half a year. Unexpectedly, the first thing we did upon meeting was exchange gifts, even though we hadn't planned it! Haha. While eating barbecue, we talked about her plans to open a hot pot restaurant, so she's been busy developing the broth. It's hard to imagine a petite girl with an adorable baby face wielding a spatula and stirring up hot pot broth with such gusto, haha. This month, while eating the chocolate peanut brittle Meng Han gave me in the office, I still occasionally think back to that long, heartfelt chatter of the night.

Lately, I've relapsed into the "bad habit" of eating chickpeas every night—the bad thing isn't the beans, it's me. It's not that the chickpeas are too delicious; it's just that my willpower is too weak. Once I opened Pandora's box (aka the chickpea jar), I could never close it again.

January is the taste of seaweed crackers, roasted chestnuts, and chickpeas; it's the frantic preparation for job hunting, the stolen moments of leisure amidst the busyness of blogging, the carefree days filled with precious memories with good friends, and also the hesitant, faint red of an unripe strawberry near the stem—

Chinese New Year is almost here.

今天是一月的最后一天，好消息——天气居然久违地放晴了。

早上吃了最爱的燕麦杯，然后中午去哼哧哼哧运动了一个小时——一个人的健身房真的很治愈…

这个月初和杉杉的新年旅行，去了一直想去的台州，吃到了好吃到惊为天人的海苔饼——外层酥脆，内里软糯，是我最爱的甜咸口味。另一个惊喜是海苔麦饼，和吃到的第一口的惊喜感一同涌上来的是后知后觉的羡慕：台州人竟然可以天天吃这么好吃的东西当作早餐。不能不提的是，在台州的大街小巷品尝了数不清的年糕变体hhh 杉杉总结：感觉一年份的年糕都在这里吃完了。我表示无辜。

想起去年的跨年夜是我和杉第一次见面，所以这趟新年旅行便显得格外有意义。除了软糯甜香的吃食之外，这次旅行也是怪奇物语之旅。每天晚上，我们都窝在酒店房间追进度，终于在从宁波回程的最后一天里把最终季画上了句号。或许在未来偶尔看到这部剧的时候，我会下意识想起海苔麦饼的香气也说不定呢hhh

还有一个好消息是，这个月我的设计店铺卖出了第一单嘿嘿（鼓掌👏）我的第一个客人是一对美国夫妇，和我联络沟通设计需求的是那位太太，在回复不及时、付款链接延时发送的情况下（已经反省过了），依然对我的设计和报价毫无挑剔…有时候真不敢相信自己的好运气——我一定在不经意间做了什么大好事，才让我遇到如此善良又宽容的客户。

月中的时候和孟寒——认识了大半年的网友——见面了。没想到见面第一个动作是交换给对方的礼物，明明没有约定好hhh吃烤肉的时候我们聊起她想要开火锅店的计划，所以最近在忙着研制底料。很难想象个子小小、拥有一张可爱娃娃脸的女生大刀阔斧挥起锅铲炒火锅底料的样子hhhh 这个月在办公室吃孟寒送的巧克力花生糖时，时不时还会想起那个大聊特聊的晚上。

最近又染上了每天晚上吃鹰嘴豆的“恶习”——恶不在豆，而在我，太好吃并不是鹰嘴豆的错，是我的意志力过于薄弱，一旦打开了潘多拉的盒子（aka鹰嘴豆罐子），就再也关不上了。

一月是海苔饼、炒栗子和鹰嘴豆的味道，是紧赶慢赶为找工作而做准备的铢积寸累，是充满热情做个人博客的忙中偷闲，是和好朋友拥有更多珍贵回忆的悠哉游哉，也是尚未完全成熟的草莓，靠近蒂头处那一抹迟疑的微红——

春节，已经悄悄靠近了。

January of 2026
